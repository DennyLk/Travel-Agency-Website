WATER TAXI

Speedboat Water Taxi

Our speedboat taxi is the best option if you want to visit one of numerous islands in the Korčula archipelago. We offer quick and affordable transportation to practically any destination you would ask for. Here are some highlights that you can visit starting from 50 kuna* pro person Children friendly sandy beach ‘Bilin žal’ in Lumbarda (Korčula island)
This is the local’s favorite beach, located on the north side of Lumbarda. The sea is crystal clear, so snorkeling around is a real pleasure. You can see the Pelješac peninsula and the islands of the Korčula archipelago from this beach. There is a small café and a restaurant right on the beach.
Island of Badija The island of Badija is situated between the Korčula and Pelješac. The island is filled with rich natural beauty. You can walk through the whole island in less than 45 minutes. It has both rocky and pebble beaches, and is therefore popular place for day trips. The island is famous for its Franciscan monastery, built in the 14th century and the church of our Virgin of Mercy. There is also a tavern and a café.
Korčula town – birthplace of Marco Polo the great world traveler and explorer
The town of Korčula is one of the best preserved medieval towns in the Mediterranean, and is traditionally the tourist and cultural centre of the Korčula island.
Moro beach Stupe island
Includes a beach bar, a pebble beach and an excellent restaurant with superb culinary experience. As they say: “We are the kind of place that doesn’t limit happy to an hour. Visit us at Stupe island and: relax, swim, laugh, eat, drink, dance, kiss…”

DAILY CRUISES

Speedboat riviera cruises

Did you know that between the island of Korčula and Pelješac peninsula there are 17 inhabited or non-inhabited islands. Get to know them close on an unforgettable excursion with one of our speed boats. Each trip can be tailored to your liking. Suitable for groups of 2-10 persons. There is a possibility of stopping for swimming, diving or refreshment at some of the exotic beach bars or restaurants on the islands.
Examples of some excursions:

Basic Riviera Tour
Type of excursion: Speed Boat Riviera Cruise
Cruising along the Korčula channel with occasional explanations of skippers and short stops for photographing, without anticipated longer breaks.
• Duration: 1 h
• Capacity: 2-10 person
• Price: from 20 EUR / person *

Standard Riviera Cruise
Type of excursion: Speed Boat Riviera Cruise
Cruise along the Korčula Channel, tour most of the islands with expert skipper comments. Stops on the way, based on the wishes of guests, for swimming, snorkeling or refreshments in one of the exciting bars or restaurants on the islands.
• Duration: 4 h
• Capacity: 2-10 person
• Price: from 40 EUR / person *

*Prices may vary depending on the season and size of the group

AIRPORT TRANSFERS

Van Transfers

We offer private transfers from and to the airports in Dubrovnik or Split. We want you to start your vacation in style; our driver will wait for you at the airport and take you directly to your accommodation without the hassle of crowded public transportation. On the way back, we will make sure to take you to the airport on time so you don’t miss your flight, using our modern, air-conditioned vehicles.
Transfer Orebić – Dubrovnik airport or vice versa; passenger car or mini van (from 170 EUR)
Transfer Orebić – Split airport or vice versa; passenger car or mini van (from 300 EUR)


Depends on the size of the group and luggage, we decide to use the van or passenger car.
Prices for all other destinations on Pelješac peninsula or Korčula island are on inquiry. For guests traveling to Korčula city or Lumbarda, there is a possibility of combined transfer, by the road to Orebić and by speed boat to the island.
Beside transfers from the airport, we can also arrange transfers from Orebić or Korčula to other destinations. Please get in touch with us and we will respond as quickly as possible!
