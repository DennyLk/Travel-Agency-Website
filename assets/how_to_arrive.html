HOW TO ARRIVE?

There is so many different ways to get to Pelješac peninsula and Korčula island...

If you take the highway from Zagreb over Split towards Dubrovnik, the highway ends in Ploče. From Ploče you will drive towards Dubrovnik, passing thru Neum, Bosnia and Herzegovina (possible custom control). Reaching the city of Ston you take the main Pelješac road (Orebić/Korčula direction) and after 65 km of scenic drive you arrive in Orebić.

UPDATE as of January/2022:
The new Pelješac bridge will be open sometimes in late spring of 2022! In that case you should be able to drive straight to us without passing the border or taking the ferry boat!

As an alternative, you can also board a car ferry in city of Ploče that sails to Trpanj. The ride Ploče – Trpanj is about one hour long, and after that it takes about 20 minutes to reach Orebić.
Ferry boat from Orebić to Dominče (small city 3 km away from Korčula) sails every hour and takes only 10 minutes.
During the main tourist season, Orebić is connected by bus lines several times a day from major centers: Dubrovnik, Split, Zagreb and Sarajevo.

Closest airport is Dubrovnik, located in small village Čilipi about 130 km away from Orebić; in the summer months very well connected with all major European cities. After arrival at the airport there is transfer possibility to Dubrovnik bus station, where you can continue your journey by bus to Orebić. Rent-a-car option is also available. Check our website for more information about transfer possibilities from Dubrovnik to Orebić and Korčula.

There is a passenger line from Split to city of Korčula by so called catamaran (catamaran – a boat with two parallel hulls). It is a very comfortable, air-conditioned ride; it takes only 2,5 hours from Split to Korčula (including the stop at island of Hvar or Brač). Despite its capacity of over 300 passengers, the reservation is mandatory in the summer season.

Ferry Boat OREBIĆ – DOMINČE (Korčula Island)
Sailing every day from port of Orebić (Pelješac peninsula) to Dominče, small village just 3 km away from city of Korčula. The line is operated by Jadrolinija with big, modern ferry boats loading over 100 vehicles and 600 passengers, crossing the channel in about 10 minutes. Even in a high season summer months there is no longer waiting on either side of the channel.
Orebić – Dominče (Korčula) sailing schedule and price list for 2022

Ferry Boat PLOČE – TRPANJ (Pelješac peninsula)
Sailing every day from Ploče (last exit on the A1 highway towards Dubrovnik) to Trpanj (Pelješac penisula), small city 21km away from Orebić. The line is operated by Jadrolinija with big, modern ferry boats loading over 130 vehicles and 1200 passengers, crossing the channel in about 55 minutes. Even in a high season summer months there is no longer waiting on either side of the channel.
Ploče – Trpanj (Pelješac) sailing schedule and price list for 2022

Passenger Service OREBIĆ – KORČULA
The boat departs from the port in Orebić and sail to the very center of Korčula. It is suitable for daily visits to the island of Korčula, because you do not need a car. The line is operated by m/b Zvijezda Kvarnera capacity of about 100 passengers and crossing the channel in 15 minutes. All information about the schedule and pricing are available at:
